Stellar Thoughts

Stellar Thoughts is a book of stories about life, love, and loss. After years of struggling and realizing that stories have the power to heal ourselves and other people, Syrine Gladys Podadera wrote this book which is a collection of all the scars she got from her bittersweet experiences.
